# 2026-01-01 — Karpathy on the First 100% Autonomous FSD Coast-to-Coast Drive

## Source

Karpathy's X post celebrating a customer-run Tesla FSD V14.2 coast-to-coast drive: **2 days 20 hours, 2,732 miles, zero interventions.**

## Key claims

- The coast-to-coast autonomous drive was "a major goal for the [[tesla-autopilot|Autopilot]] team from the start."
- Years of "marathon clip review sessions late into the night" went into triaging interventions, categorizing them, and closing gaps.
- Karpathy's preference: the drive being done by **a customer with a production release** is "the right way" — more authentic than a tuned Tesla demo run.

## Why it matters for the wiki

This is the empirical payoff on the [[march-of-nines]]:

- In 2013 the first [[waymo|Waymo]] demo ride was flawless (source: Dwarkesh interview).
- In 2026 a Tesla customer drives coast-to-coast on FSD V14.2 with zero interventions.
- The "demo is 90% of the way" line is specifically about the prior 12 years between first flawless demo and first end-to-end production-drive success.

## Quotes

> "Amazing to see the system actually get there and huge congrats to the team!"
