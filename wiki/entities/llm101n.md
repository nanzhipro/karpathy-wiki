# LLM101n

Karpathy's announced full-LLM course for his Eureka education project. The proposed sequel to [[zero-to-hero]]: an end-to-end walk from tokens to a working small LLM, packaged as a single coherent curriculum rather than a YouTube playlist.

## Role

- The flagship public product of [[eureka|Eureka]].
- Intended as a **ramp from nothing to a real LLM**, combining code-first pedagogy with the [[ramps-to-knowledge|ramp]] philosophy.
- Delivery: course format with lectures, exercises, and buildable code artifacts.

## Mentions in sources

- [Karpathy, Berkeley SkyDeck Keynote](../sources/berkeley-ai-hackathon-2024-keynote.md) — announced as upcoming.

## Status as of sources

Announced but not shipped at the time of the 2024 keynote. Karpathy treats it as the culminating artifact of Eureka rather than a side project.
