# Prime Intellect

Decentralized-compute and RL-environments startup. Referenced by Karpathy in [[karpathy-x-2025-rl-and-learning-paradigms|May 11 2025]] and subsequent posts as the canonical example of **"environments as the new data."**

## What they do

- **Environments Hub** — a registry of reproducible RL training environments (math, code, browser, terminal, games).
- **Decentralized training** — distributed GPU pooling for contributors.
- **INTELLECT-series models** — trained in part using their own environment catalog.

## Why Karpathy highlights them

The [[karpathy-x-2025-rl-and-learning-paradigms|May 11]] thesis: the next scaling input isn't more pretraining text, it's more **environments**. The company building the distribution layer for environments is playing the role GitHub played for code.

> Pretraining = imitation over internet text.
> Post-training RL = imitation-and-improvement over environments.
>
> If you don't have environments, you don't have a post-training story.

## Relation to other concepts

- **[[rlvr]]** — Prime Intellect's environments are exactly the verifiable-reward substrate RLVR needs.
- **[[verifiability]]** — the environment catalog is the catalog of verifiable tasks.
- **[[karpathy-x-2025-software-paradigm|Environments are the new data]]** — the 2025 paradigm statement Prime Intellect is building under.
