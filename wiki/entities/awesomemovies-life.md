# awesomemovies.life

A student-built movie-recommendation web app shown at the Berkeley SkyDeck hackathon Karpathy keynoted in 2024. Karpathy cited it as a concrete example of the new kind of weekend-built, LLM-powered product the audience should aim for.

## What it is

- A small web app that recommends movies using an LLM in the loop.
- Built by a hackathon participant in the spirit of a [[snowballs|snowball]] project.

## Why it's in the corpus

- Used by Karpathy to make the "feel the AGI, then build" pitch concrete. The demo isn't special in itself — it's special as *proof that a student could build it*.
- Foreshadows the [[vibe-coding]] coinage he'd publish later.
