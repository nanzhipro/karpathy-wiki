# Llama

**Type:** Product — Meta's family of open-weight large language models.

## Relevance to this wiki

In [[software-is-changing-again]], Karpathy positions Llama as the **Linux of the [[llm-os]]**. The analogy:

- Closed-source providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) are the "Windows / macOS" — integrated, polished, proprietary.
- Open-weight ecosystems anchored on Llama parallel the Linux ecosystem: permissive licensing, community forks, a supply chain of fine-tunes, quantizations, and derivative models.
- Karpathy's bet: "Llama is possibly going to grow into something like Linux" — i.e. the open-source alternative that wins the long game across specific deployments (on-device, regulated industries, cost-sensitive workloads).
