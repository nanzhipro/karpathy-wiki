# nanochat

**Type:** Open-source project (Karpathy, 2025) — an end-to-end LLM codebase ("~8000 lines of code") that trains a chat model from scratch and serves it, intended as a "strong baseline" [[ramps-to-knowledge|ramp to knowledge]].

## Relevance to this wiki

- Referenced in [[summoning-ghosts-not-animals]] as one of Karpathy's teaching ramps. The positioning: existing "nano" repos cover pretraining ([[nanogpt]]) or automatic differentiation ([[micrograd]]); nanochat covers the *full* chat-model pipeline including supervised fine-tuning and serving.
- Sibling to [[llm-c]] in the ramp hierarchy: llm.c shows what's under the framework; nanochat shows the full lifecycle from raw text to deployed chat.
- One of the templates Karpathy has in mind for [[eureka|Eureka]]'s curriculum.
