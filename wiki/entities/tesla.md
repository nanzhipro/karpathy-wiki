# Tesla

**Type:** Company — electric-vehicle manufacturer; operator of Tesla Autopilot / Full Self-Driving.

## Relevance to this wiki

Karpathy led Tesla's vision/autopilot team 2017–2022. Tesla Autopilot is the deep prior behind several Karpathy frames:

- **Software 2.0 exemplar.** In [[software-is-changing-again]], Karpathy shows Autopilot as a system where neural-net weights progressively *eat* hand-written C++ — a canonical [[software-3-0|Software 1.0 → 2.0]] transition.
- **[[autonomy-slider]] and [[march-of-nines]].** The Tesla experience ("perfect drive delivered on the first day, 12 years later Karpathy still intervenes") is the empirical base for his insistence that agentic AI products keep humans in the loop and grow autonomy conservatively. See [Summoning Ghosts](../sources/summoning-ghosts-not-animals.md) for the most detailed treatment.
- **PyTorch rewrite anecdote.** [Berkeley 2024 keynote](../sources/berkeley-ai-hackathon-2024-keynote.md) — Karpathy describes the 2017 Tesla rewrite from Lua/Torch to PyTorch as "a weekend project" that became load-bearing infrastructure.
