# MatX

AI accelerator startup building LLM-specialized chips. Referenced by Karpathy on Feb 21 2026 in his note on the **"tsunami of tokens"** — the expectation that token throughput, not peak TFLOPs, is the binding constraint of the agent era.

## Karpathy's take

With agents generating and consuming tokens at orders-of-magnitude higher volume than human-in-the-loop chatting, throughput-focused silicon (MatX and peers) is where the economic leverage moves.
