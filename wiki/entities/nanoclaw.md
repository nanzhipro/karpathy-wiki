# nanoclaw

Karpathy-referenced minimal implementation of the emerging [[claws|claw]] orchestration layer — the "nano" treatment applied to agent orchestration in the spirit of [[nanogpt]] and [[nanochat]].

## Purpose

A pedagogical, rippable demo of what a "claw" — a layer that composes, routes, and supervises multiple agents and skills — looks like when stripped to the essentials. Positioned as a reference artifact for people building their own orchestration rather than adopting a heavyweight framework.

## Relation to Claws

See [[claws]] for the broader concept. `nanoclaw` is to claws what microGPT is to GPT: the smallest working illustration.
