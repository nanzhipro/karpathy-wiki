# Simile

A startup building **LLM-based population simulators** — systems that compose many agent personae into simulated societies for market research, policy testing, and narrative playtesting.

## Karpathy's Feb 13 2026 note

Karpathy highlights Simile as one of the first products chasing the **"agent networks"** frame: value coming not from a single capable agent but from *populations* of interacting agents. Consistent with his 150K-agent "moltbook" thought experiment on Feb 4.
