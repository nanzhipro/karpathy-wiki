# Hugging Face

The de-facto hub for open-source ML models, datasets, and tooling. Functions as the distribution layer for the open side of the [[llm-os]] stack.

## Why it appears in the Karpathy corpus

- **[[vibe-coding]] context.** In the YC talk, Karpathy cites Hugging Face CEO Tom Wolf using the term "vibe coding" in a high-school presentation — a marker that the coinage has fully crossed into mainstream industry vocabulary.
- **Distribution of weights.** Open-model releases ([[llama|LLaMA]], DeepSeek, etc.) live on Hugging Face; if there is a fab-like analog to OSS models, Hugging Face is the wafer distributor.

## Position in the stack

- Not a frontier-lab peer of [[openai|OpenAI]] or Anthropic.
- Adjacent to it: the neutral meeting place where weights, datasets, demos, and docs converge.
