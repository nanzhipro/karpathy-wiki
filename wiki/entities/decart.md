# Decart

Israeli AI startup. Creator of **MirageLSD**, the real-time diffusion system Karpathy flagged in [[karpathy-x-2025-video-gen|Jul 18 2025]] as the inflection point for live generative video.

## MirageLSD

A diffusion model engineered for **≥30 fps interactive video generation** — cheap enough per frame that the model becomes a usable rendering engine rather than a batch tool.

The unlocks Karpathy listed:
- Real-time video filters with generative content
- Live in-world AR overlays
- Interactive generated environments (games, virtual production)
- Livestreaming with generative augmentation

## Why it matters

Most 2025 video gen (Veo 3, Sora 2, Kling) was *offline batch* — minute-per-second render times, great for pre-production. MirageLSD is the **first credibly real-time** entry. That flips the deployment surface:

- Batch video → editing, ads, film
- Real-time video → games, AR, live broadcast, XR
