# Eureka

**Type:** Company — Karpathy's current AI-education venture. Pitched in [[summoning-ghosts-not-animals]] as a "Starfleet Academy" / "gym for the mind" — an institution designed around LLMs as 1-1 tutors at scale.

## Thesis (from the Dwarkesh interview)

- **Bloom's two-sigma** is the aspiration: students taught 1-1 outperform classroom-taught peers by ~2σ. The bottleneck has always been supply of great tutors. LLMs are the first technology that can make that supply effectively infinite.
- The hard design problem is **pedagogy**, not model capability. Most educational AI products today surface the LLM directly and leave the student to drive. Eureka's bet is on authored curricula that use the LLM as an execution engine — building [[ramps-to-knowledge]] rather than chat.
- Karpathy's own open-source teaching ramps ([[micrograd]], [[nanogpt]], [[nanochat]], [[llm-c]], [[cs231n]], [[zero-to-hero]]) are the template.

## Status

Early-stage as of October 2025. Karpathy describes it as the project he's spending most of his attention on post-OpenAI.
