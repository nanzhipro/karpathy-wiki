# OpenAI

**Type:** Company — AI research lab and product company. Karpathy is a founding member (2015); departed, returned (2023–2024), departed again.

## Relevance to this wiki

- **Frontier-model provider** — GPT-5 Pro, ChatGPT, Advanced Voice Mode, and [[openai-codex|Codex]] are repeatedly cited across sources.
- **Closed-source leader in the [[llm-os]] analogy** — in [[software-is-changing-again]], Karpathy maps GPT-family models to "Windows / macOS" of the new OS stack; the Linux counterpart is [[llama|Llama]].
- **Karpathy's employment history** — founding context matters for interpreting his takes on OpenAI-adjacent products (e.g. his characterization of Advanced Voice Mode as "slightly orphaned" in [AI Capability Gap](../sources/ai-capability-gap.md)).
